Nestled amidst the picturesque landscapes of central Alberta, Canada, Cherry Grove exudes a charming, small-town allure.

This delightful hamlet, a hidden gem within the Municipal District of Bonnyville No. 87, sits just off of Highway 55. Cherry Grove offers a perfect blend of serenity and accessibility for both locals and visitors alike.

  • Population (2014): 405
  • 12 km Southeast Cold Lake
