Site Details

Site Name

I have chosen "Cherry Grove Chamber of Commerce" as my site name, it's simple and to the point. People know what to expect from this type of site based on it's name so it represents the purpose well.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to showcase Cherry Grove and its' services. Cherry Grove is a small outlying hamlet outside of the City of Cold Lake. It has it's own hall, ice rink, rodeo grounds, and has some small businesses and services. We're a small community and growing. Getting the news out of all the businesses and services available in our area will help people to take advantage of our local services.

The second purpose is to have a place that will help the community advertise our activities and calendars, so it will help the community to connect with one another.
